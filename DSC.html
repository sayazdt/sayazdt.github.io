Data Science and Covid-19

By Hossein Sarempour Covid, Data, Science Contact

When it was claimed that "patient zero" was found in Wuhan, China in Dec. 2019 nobody could predict that it would be a pandemic very soon especially after the whole of Wuhan was quarantined. So the world faced a catastrophe which it wasn't equipped for. As the Corona infected the worldwide except Antarctica, technology tries to assist the medical science to find a solution to eradicate such problem. One of the tools was data science.
Data science with computational biology, statistical analysis, and computer science together have played an important role to cope with this unwelcomed guest through collecting data from models, mathematical models, correlations, and predictive models which developed for Corona. The datasets sources can be potentially used in diagnostics and evaluation of health strategies.
We use data science especially data analytics to monitor real-time disease outbreak, predicting and spotting real-time trends to report health organizations.
Data scientists collate information from social media and patient records, after analyzing them, they try to forecast the trends, help policymakers and inform society to cope with the challenge better.
In your opinion what is the other thing data science is able to do?!
