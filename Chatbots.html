Chatbots

By SAYAZ Bot, Chat, NLP Contact

A chatbot is a software application that is used to conduct online chat conversations via text or text-to-speech, rather than providing direct contact with on-site human agents. The chatbot system is designed to convincingly simulate the behavior of humans as conversation partners. It usually requires constant adjustment and testing. Many systems in production still cannot fully talk or pass the industry standard Turing test.

Chatbots are increasingly appearing in enterprises and are often used to automate tasks that do not require skilled personnel. As customer service takes place through messaging applications and phone calls, there are more and more use cases where chatbot deployments provide organizations with a clear return on investment. Call center workers may be particularly vulnerable to AI-driven chatbots.

Chatbots are important as they can work instead of most of chat operators in any kind of businesses. They can be used registeration bots, they can be used as suppport bots, They can be used as HR bots, They also can be used as algorithmic bots that can perform specific algorithms.
